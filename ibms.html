Intelligent Building Management System (IBMS)

An Intelligent Building Management System (IBMS) is an advanced, integrated system designed to centralize the control and automation of a building’s essential operations. It acts as the “brain” of modern buildings, optimizing performance, safety, and comfort while reducing operational costs and energy consumption.

Key Components

  • HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) Control
  • Lighting Control
  • Security & Access Control
  • Fire & Life Safety Systems
  • Energy Management
